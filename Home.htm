About

Our Story Make History

    About Chef Wondi Meet Chef Wondi, a seasoned chef with over 45 years of experience in the culinary world. Chef Wondi started his journey in the kitchen at a very young age. Despite his fervent love for cooking, the road to success has not been an easy one. After spending more than a decade on a ship as a captain steward, he migrated to Canada and worked for 34 years at various Hotels. To further develop his carrier in the culinary world, Chef Wondi attended and graduated from the esteemed George Brown University, the center for Hospitality and Culinary Arts. He continued to excel in his profession as a self-taught individual who continuously nurtured his skill for crafting unique dishes. After mastering culinary arts and techniques, he has now perfected the exquisite dishes of both Ethiopian and foreign cuisines. He now particularly specializes on catering service for different events. Chef Wondi is an enthusiastic patriot, who is avid an advocate of Ethiopian culture and foods. Serving abroad with such special endowment of catering and culinary has not changed his ambition for return back to his roots. He strongly believes that Ethiopia’s cuisine has a strong potential to be an attraction for tourists and to compete among other dishes worldwide. After making intensive research on the situation of Ethiopia in general and the tourism sector in particular, Chef Wondiy has been inspired to open “Chef Wendi Culinary art Institution”, which specializes on gastronomy and focuses on the culinary art education of tomorrow’s food professionals. It accommodates one-to-one private students who wish to study in culinary and hospitality events. In the upcoming future, the institution aims to establish itself as one of the top institutions by producing top tier professionals in the culinary world.
